My favourite attractions in San Diego

San Diego Zoo

San Diego Zoo Wildlife Alliance is an international, nonprofit conservation organization with two front doors: the San Diego Zoo and the San Diego Zoo Safari Park. We integrate wildlife health and care, science, and education to develop sustainable conservation solutions.

Address:

2920 Zoo Drive, San Diego, CA 92101

What I like about it

An amazing place to visit. The Zoo has a strong conservationist approach, which appeals to me, and is a fantastic day out. From elephants and giraffes to hippos, lions and even red pandas, you'll have the chance to see incredible wildlife up close.

Petco Park

Home to the Padres, Petco Park is a stunning stadium, combining the best sight lines in baseball with breathtaking views of San Diego. Cheer on the Padres in the MLB National League, and learn about the history of the club and the stadium on one of the daily tours.

Address:

100 Park Blvd. San Diego, CA 92101

What I like about it

If you want the All-American experience, you have to catch a baseball game! Lend your support to the Padres and get involved - the action's not just on the field, but off it, with tempting snacks such as tacos, carnitas, sushi, and of course pizza and hot dogs to choose from!
