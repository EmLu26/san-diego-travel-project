Why San Diego?

America's 'Finest City'

San Diego (Spanish for 'Saint Didacus') is a city located in the extreme south-west of the U.S. state of California. With over 1.3 million residents, the city is the eighth-most populous in the USA. San Diego Zoo

San Diego Zoo Wildlife Alliance is an international, nonprofit conservation organization with two front doors: the San Diego Zoo and the San Diego Zoo Safari Park. We integrate wildlife health and care, science, and education to develop sustainable conservation solutions.

Address:

2920 Zoo Drive, San Diego, CA 92101

What I like about it

An amazing place to visit and see wildlife up close. The Zoo has a strong conservationist approach, which appeals to me, and is a fantastic day out. From elephants and giraffes to hippos, lions and even red pandas, you'll have the chance to see incredible wildlife up close.
